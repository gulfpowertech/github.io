About Us

Leading Electromechanical Contractor in UAE & KSA

Gulf Solution Power Company LLC is a leading electrical and contracting company established in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. As part of a reputable group of companies — including Gulf Power Electromechanical Contracting L.L.C, Royal Power General Contracting, and Asian Power Electromechanical Works Contracting — we are recognized as an approved contractor across UAE and KSA.

We specialize in delivering comprehensive solutions for electrical, mechanical, plumbing(MEP) and fire protection systemswith experience supporting electrical infrastructure projects across the power sector in the kindgom of Saudi Arabia.

Our core expertise include the supply, installation, operation, maintenance, testing, and commissioning of electrical systems up to 33kV, with a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
